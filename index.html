About

I am a CS undergraduate with broad interests in multimodal foundation models, geometric reasoning, and embodied AI. My recent work focuses on pushing 3D-LLMs toward more reliable, geometry-grounded perception through contrastive decoding. I am also interested in the intersection of RL and VLM/VLA systems, especially how robots can integrate language, 3D vision, and action.

Beyond technical work, I enjoy running, science fiction, and writing about research ideas in clear and structured forms. I value rigor, clarity, and building systems that combine theoretical soundness with real-world usability.

Skills: Python, PyTorch, Git, MindSpore, RL frameworks

Research Interests: 3D-LLMs, VLM/VLA, geometric reasoning, RL, embodied agents
